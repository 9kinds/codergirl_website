About Us

LaunchCoderGirl is a LaunchCode community program that is an all-women's coding meet-up.
CoderGirl provides a space for women of all skill levels to explore topics in programming and technology and be supported by a community of like-minded individuals. This meet-up is an opportunity for women to network, learn, and create.

Come join us! Women of all ages, backgrounds, and skill levels are welcome! We meet every Wednesday @ 4240 Duncan Ave, St. Louis, MO 63110 on the 2nd floor in the Cambridge Innovation Community (CIC) from 6 p.m. to 8 p.m.

<

CoderGirls are exploring a variety of topics, these include but are not limited to:

  • Harvard's CS50 Introduction to Computer Science Course
  • Java with Jenny! An Introductory Course to Java with CoderGirl Mentor Jenny Brown
  • Arduino Hardware Hackers with CoderGirl Mentor Jessica du Maine
  • SQL Servers and Databases with CoderGirl Mentor Kathi Kellenberger
  • Web Development and Design with CoderGirl Mentors Helena, Rachel, and Rosie
